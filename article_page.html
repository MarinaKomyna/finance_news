Finance tradenews Partnered with NewsCrypto to Launch Crypto School Program

  • 2020/04/16 06:29:31
  • NEWS

On April 16, 2020, KuCoin announced that it has reached a cooperation with the crypto information educational platform NewsCrypto. Together, they will launch a series of blockchain-related education courses aimed at promoting blockchain technology and drive its mass adoption as a result.

The lessons launched by KuCoin and NewsCrypto cover different groups of people, including zero-based groups, users with certain investment experiences, and expert investors. From the education courses, users can find basic crypto knowledge, learn how to read charts, get to know popular tools and indicators and learn how to use them, etc. Users will be able to complete a few tasks after each lesson and get the Certificate of Completion.

“Any investment is accompanied by risk. As the People’s Exchange, I hope that every user participating in the market can fully understand the relevant knowledge of crypto, recognize the potential risks of the market, and manage it effectively," KuCoin Global CEO Johnny Lyu said. "Crypto is changing rapidly, and we will continue to launch new courses to meet your learning and investment needs, and promote the mass adoption of blockchain technology and crypto in the next 5-10 years."

KuCoin is one of the most popular crypto exchanges. Now, KuCoin offers financial services including fiat-to-crypto, crypto-to-crypto, futures, staking, borrowing, token launch and more to its 5 million users across 207 countries and regions around the world. One out of four crypto holders worldwide are with KuCoin.

NewsCrypto aims to be the first educational platform that includes all types of crypto transactions, providing users with important transaction information and trading tools. NewsCrypto hopes to guide and help every user to make a reasonable investment decision.
